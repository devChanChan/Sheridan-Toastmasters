Sheridan Toastmasters is the ideal campus club for self - improvement with a focus on communication and leadership. Club chapters are located in all three Sheridan campuses.

Becoming one of us is

as easy as 1, 2, 3

1. Visit Our Meetings
We hold weekly meetings at each campus. Come see how we operate and if you would like to be with us.
2. Become a Member
We offer the most affordable membership fee of any Toastmasters Clubs around the area, as low as $10/month.
3. Set Your Learning Goals
Our VP of Education will help you create your own personalized learning path and pacing that is most effective for your personal growth.
